### resync

#### CFTPROT

****\[RESYNC = {<span style="text-decoration: underline;">NO</span>
| YES }\]****

Option for dynamic re-synchronization of exchanges during transfer,
without interrupting the data exchange phase, negotiated during the connection
phase.
